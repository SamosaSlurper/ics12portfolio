Looking Ahead: Every boy can dream

🌟

My 10-Year Vision

Realistically 10 years from now I pray that I end up as a financial analyst, with a degree in accounting and finance (masters or phd), with a loving equal to me and be living my life to my heart’s desires. Obviously though, like most people, I probably wouldn’t mind becoming richer than Elon Musk (I might change my name to Melon Husk then).

One thing I’m looking forward to in life is just settling down with a loving family, great friends, a sturdy job and looking out the window wondering how I ever got there. I’d look back in time however as I recount my life and thank my lucky stars that I had amazing teachers and amazing parents to bring me to what I hopefully will become.
